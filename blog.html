Unicode Text Guide

Understanding Unicode is key to mastering fancy text. Unicode is a global standard that assigns a unique number to every character across all writing systems. When you see fancy text online, you're actually seeing standard Unicode characters that just look different.

Our tool maps each Latin letter to equivalent-looking characters from other Unicode blocks. This is why fancy text works across different platforms and devices without needing any special fonts installed.

The mathematical alphanumeric block (U+1D400-U+1D7FF) provides most of our styles, including bold, italic, script, fraktur, double-struck, and monospace variations.
