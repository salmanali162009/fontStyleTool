Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Fancy Text Generator respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. All text processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not store, transmit, or have access to the text you enter.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save your preferences (such as dark/light mode), favorite fonts, and recently copied items. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party advertising networks to display ads. These networks may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads. We do not control the data collection practices of these third parties.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies for basic functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that some features may not work properly with cookies disabled.

Data Security

Since all text processing happens client-side in your browser, no data is transmitted to our servers. This means your text and generated fonts remain private to you.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
